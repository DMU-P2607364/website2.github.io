Trippie Redd - Miss The Rage feat. Playboi Carti Review

This recent release is an interesting one for current Trippie and Carti fans. Trippie was nice, with the instumental and him working well together.

Never the less, Carti's verse wasn't the best, with the original leaked verse being alot better. I will give this single a 3.5/5, could be improved but a nice song!
